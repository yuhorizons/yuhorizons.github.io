# Bronte Creek  

## info  
* Things to do around Bronte Creek campground

## steps  
1. Take Hwy 407 west
2. Take Hwy 407 towards Hamilton
3. Take Bronte Rd Exit (25)
4. Turn right on Upper Middle Rd W
5. Go stright into Bronte Creek campground
