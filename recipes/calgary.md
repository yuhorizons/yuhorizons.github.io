# Calgary Road Trip  

## info  
* 35 hr drive from Toronto to Calgary

## steps  
1. Toronto
2. London
3. Lansing
4. LakeExpress High-Speed Ferry
5. Minneapolis-Sleep (15 hr drive from Toronto) 
6. Alexandria
7. Fargo
8. Minot
9. Estevan
10. Regina-Sleep (12 hr drive from Minneapolis)
11. Swift Current
12. Medicine Hat
13. Calgary (8 hr drive from Regina)
