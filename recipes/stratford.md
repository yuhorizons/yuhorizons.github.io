# Stratford  

## info  
* Road trip from Toronto to Stratford

## steps  
1. Drive to waterloo (Hwy 401, exit Hwy 8)
2. Take Hwy 8 to stratford
3. Exit Foundry St. North to visit Castle Kilbride

## notes

*  Castle Kilbride (60 Snyder's Rd W, Baden, ON N3A 1A1)
